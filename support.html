FAQs

How do I book an item?

Open an item, tap Book Now, select your dates, and send a request to the owner.

How do I add my item?

Go to the Add tab, fill in details (title, price/day, category, photo), then publish.

Payments and refunds?

Payments are handled by our provider. Refunds depend on the owner’s policy and rental status.

Report a problem

Email support@hyrahemma.com with your booking ID and screenshots.
